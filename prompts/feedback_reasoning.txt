- V is the volume,
- n is the number of moles,
- R is the universal gas constant (0.0821, \text{L}, \text{atm} \, \text{K}^{-1} \, \text{mol}^{-1}),
- T is the absolute temperature in Kelvin.

T = 300.15 \, \text{K}
